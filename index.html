Why AltBeacon?

Currently there is no good open, free, or libre standard for working with proximity beacons across platforms and devices. Radius Networks saw this need and has worked to author an open specification. Radius authored this first version of the spec; in the future, this spec will be managed by a not-for-profit organization.

We firmly believe that an open standard would help everyone, and want to do it right. This is the proposal, we would like feedback from the community

What is AltBeacon?

  • The AltBeacon standard is available for all to read and implement.
  • The AltBeacon standard is intended to create a fair, competitive market for implementations of the standard. They do not lock the customer into a particular vendor or group.
  • The AltBeacon standard is free for all to implement, with no royalty or fee.
