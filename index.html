Why AltBeacon?

Currently there is no good open and interoperable specification for working with proximity beacons across platforms and devices. Radius Networks saw this need and has worked to author an open specification.

We firmly believe that an open specification would help everyone, and want to do it right. This is the proposal, we would like feedback from the community

What is AltBeacon?

  • The AltBeacon specification is intended to create a fair, competitive market for implementations of the specification. They do not lock the customer into a particular vendor or group.
  • The AltBeacon specification is free for all to implement, with no royalty or fee.
  • AltBeacon does not favor one implementor over another for any reason other than the technical standards compliance of a vendor's implementation.
